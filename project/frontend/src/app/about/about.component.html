The aim of this text is to provide a first description of the The Open Network (TON) and related blockchain, peer-to-peer, distributed storage and service hosting technologies. To reduce the size of this document to reasonable proportions, we focus mainly on the unique and dening features of the TON platform that are important for it to achieve its stated goals. One might think about TON as a huge distributed supercomputer, or rather a huge superserver, intended to host and provide a variety of services. This text is not intended to be the ultimate reference with respect to all implementation details. Some particulars are likely to change during the development and testing phases.
