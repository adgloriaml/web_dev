Recipe #1

  • Time: 1 hr 30 mins
  • Serves: 9
Ingredients Quantity
frozen mixed berries 4 cups
white sugar 0.5 cups
cornstarch 1 tablespoon
salt 1 pinch

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x9-inch pan.

  2. Toss together frozen berries (do not thaw first), sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, and salt. Pour into the prepared pan.

  3. Mix together flour and yogurt in a medium bowl. Knead dough with your hands a few times until smooth.

  4. Place pan into preheated oven and bake until topping is browned, and the fruit mixture in the center of the pan is bubbling, 55 to 60 minutes.
